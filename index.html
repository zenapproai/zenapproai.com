How to start

You need to download Meow Six APP from Google Play Store. We only provide APPLE Testflight version of Meow Six APP for iOS use.

You can design your audio APP in Meow Six APP directly. The APP uses 'what you see what you get' approach to show your design output and provides method to output your APK file into Android phone for testing. For iOS testing, you need to join Apple Developer Program and create signed ipa. You need to use APPLE Testflight APP to install the signed ipa on your own iPhone.

Features of playlist and lyrics are supported by this APP. About Audio file caching, token authorization of server-side audio files and playlist data from Internet source, please feel free to contact us to get version 2.0 or 2.0.2 of Meow Six Template which is in beta and is planned to release very soon. The token is about using Google sign-in and Apple sign-in to get time restricted token from your server.

Consideration of audio file caching advantage to server loading and memory consumption amount of user phone due to the caching is needed if your APP uses version 2.0.2 of Meow Six Template.

Meow Six APP

You can use this APP to design and preview your own audio APP. In your APP, background picture applying and custom colors of its audio player are designable. The most simplest steps of APP design are only to prepare a portrait photo and few audio files and pack them with APP codes to build APP file. In order to show specific style and rich content of your APP, you are recommended to design html website as APP landing page and place your audio files on Internet server.

'Three APP types' strategy are recommended. Pure audio APP is for target audience. Mixed content APP contains specific content sector with audio playlist functions for user classification in sponsor marketing use. Tourist APP contains specific touring content area with audio playlist functions and is to boost overall installation rate.

We also provide ZENAPPRO Template about user interface (UI) of APP and development of content typed APP. Please feel free to contact us to get one.

Before APP design, you need to know the requirements of Play Store and APP Store, particularly in APP size limitation and minimum iOS supported version / Android API level.
