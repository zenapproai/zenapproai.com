How to start

Please download MeowSixAPP from the Google Play Store. For iOS APP designers, we currently provide the Apple Internal Test version (via Apple TestFlight APP), which can be used to preview your app content.

You can design your audio app directly within MeowSixAPP. The app uses a 'What You See Is What You Get' (WYSIWYG) approach to display your design output. For Android, you can export an APK file directly to your phone for testing. For iOS testing, you must join the Apple Developer Program to create a signed IPA file, which can then be installed on your iPhone using the Apple TestFlight app.

The app now supports playlist and lyrics features. Regarding server-side token authorization for audio files and internet-sourced playlist data, please contact us for access to MeowSixAPP Template version 2.0 or 2.0.2. This version is currently in beta and scheduled for imminent release. It implements Google and Apple Sign-in to generate time-restricted access tokens from your server.

MeowSixAPP

Use MeowSixAPP to design and preview your own custom audio applications. You can fully customize the audio player's color scheme and background imagery to match your brand. The simplest design workflow involves bundling a single portrait photo and a few audio files with the app’s source code to generate an app installation file. For a more sophisticated user experience and richer content, we recommend designing a custom HTML website as your app’s starting page and hosting your audio files on a remote server.

'We recommend three distinct app strategies. Pure audio APP is designed for a dedicated target audience seeking a focused listening experience. Mixed content APP features a specific content sector integrated with audio playlist functions. This model is ideal for user segmentation and sponsored marketing, allowing brands to reach niche listener groups. Tourist APP combines travel-specific content with audio playlists (such as audio guides). This strategy is designed to maximize installation rates by offering high-utility, location-based value.

We also provide ZENAPPRO Template about user interface (UI) of APP and development of content typed APP. Please feel free to contact us to get one.

Before designing your application, it is essential to understand the current requirements for the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. Pay close attention to app size limitations, the minimum supported iOS version, and the required Android target API level.
